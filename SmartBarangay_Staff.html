📜 Terms & Conditions — Smart Barangay Staff Portal [ View Full Terms ]
1. PURPOSE

Smart Barangay Starita is an official digital platform for authorized barangay personnel. Use is strictly limited to duly appointed barangay officials and staff of Barangay Starita, Olongapo City.

2. AUTHORIZED ACCESS ONLY

Access is role-based. Unauthorized access or credential sharing is prohibited under Republic Act No. 10175 (Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2012).

3. DATA PRIVACY

All resident information is protected under Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012). Staff must not disclose resident data to unauthorized parties.

4. ACCEPTABLE USE

System must be used exclusively for official barangay functions. All activities are logged and monitored.

5. ACCOUNT SECURITY

You are responsible for the confidentiality of your credentials. Immediately report unauthorized access to the system administrator.
