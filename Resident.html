Terms & Conditions — SmartBarangay

1
Purpose & Scope

The SmartBarangay Resident Portal is an official digital platform provided by Barangay Sta. Rita, Olongapo City to facilitate AI-assisted incident reporting, real-time community communication, and transparent public safety management. By registering, you agree to use this platform solely for lawful, community-beneficial purposes.

2
Data Privacy

All personal information collected is governed by the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act 10173). Your data is stored securely, never sold to third parties, and used only to process reports and improve barangay services.

3
Incident Reporting

Residents must submit accurate, truthful reports. False reports, harassment, or malicious use of the AI incident system may result in account suspension and legal action under applicable Philippine law.

4
AI-Assisted Features

The AI analysis tools are advisory in nature. Final decisions regarding incident response are made by authorized barangay officials.

5
Emergency Reporting

The emergency quick-report feature is for genuine emergencies only. For life-threatening situations, always contact 911 or your local emergency services first.

6
Image Upload Policy

Uploaded images must be relevant to the reported incident. Uploading obscene, defamatory, or misleading images is strictly prohibited under RA 10175 (Cybercrime Prevention Act).

7
Account Security

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials. Report unauthorized access to the barangay IT office immediately.

8
Changes to Terms

The Barangay reserves the right to update these terms at any time. Continued use of the portal constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
